About Us

Locally owned and operated for 27 years.

Tucson Landscape Lighting has called the Old Pueblo home for nearly three decades. Co-owners Johnny Lohrman and Mike Lopez lead a team that designs, installs, maintains, and repairs low-voltage landscape lighting across Southern Arizona — from the Catalina Foothills and Dove Mountain to Oro Valley, SaddleBrooke, and now the Phoenix area.

As a Licensed, Bonded and Insured Arizona contractor (ROC #306909), we use resort-style, commercial-grade AMP fixtures and transformers backed by a manufacturer's lifetime warranty — built to last in the desert and easy to maintain for years to come.

"We will provide the highest quality customer service and premium quality lighting options to the Arizona community with excellence, integrity, and respect."
